**Speaker:** LTJG Joram Stith  
**Topic:** Cyber Protection Teams (CPT) & the Marine Transportation System (MTS)

---

## CPT Role in the MTS
Cyber Protection Teams are deployable Coast Guard units providing cybersecurity services to the Marine Transportation System (MTS). Their mission includes:

- **Assess** – Deliver evaluations, guidance, and recommendations to protect MTS networks.  
- **Hunt** – Identify malicious actors or undetected adversaries operating within MTS systems.  
- **Service** – Provide specialized cybersecurity support and mission-driven technical services.

---

## Mission Types

### Assess
- Identify vulnerabilities and misconfigurations.  
- Provide cybersecurity recommendations to improve resilience.

### Hunt
- Detect adversarial activity before it impacts critical operations.  
- Conduct proactive threat discovery.

### Incident Response
- Provide mitigation and remediation advice.  
- Conduct forensic analysis.  
- Assist with coordination across federal partners (FBI, CISA, etc.).

---

## DMSS Kit
Deployable hardware and software used by CPTs for assessments, hunts, and incident response.

## OCEAN Management
- Backbone infrastructure for CPT mission support.  
- Enables data ingestion, correlation, and threat analysis.  
- Provides an analyst-focused operational environment.

## Junior Officer (JO) Opportunities
- Opportunities to serve as **change-makers** in Coast Guard cyberspace operations.  
- Direct interaction and mission support with the **Department of Defense (DoD)**.  
- Access to advanced **education and certifications**.  
- Open to officers across numerous operational and technical backgrounds.

# 3. DoD Cyber Guest Lecture
**Speaker:** 1LT Lehmmer or sumn like that  

---

## DODIN Operations
Actions required to secure, operate, extend, maintain, and sustain DoD cyberspace and the Department of Defense Information Network (DODIN).

---

## Cyberspace Mission Types

### Defensive Cyberspace Operations (DCO)
- Protect blue-space networks from active or imminent threats.

### Offensive Cyberspace Operations (OCO)
- Project power in and through adversary networks.

---

## Cyber Units Overview

### Defense-Oriented Units
- **Cyber Protection Teams (CPTs)** – DCO-Internal Defensive Measures  
- **National Mission Teams (NMTs)** – DCO-Response Actions  
- **National Support Teams (NSTs)** – Support for national cyber missions  

### Offense-Oriented Units
- **Combat Mission Teams (CMTs)** – OCO Operations  
- **Combat Support Teams (CSTs)** – OCO Support Roles  

---

## Cyber Mission Force (CMF)

- **Cyber National Mission Force (CNMF) Headquarters**  
  - Leads operations to defeat significant cyberspace threats to the DODIN.

- **Joint Force Headquarters–Cyber (JFHQ-C)**  
  - Provides command and control of cyber forces in support of Combatant Commands.

- **Joint Force Headquarters–DODIN (JFHQ-DODIN)**  
  - Directs global defensive operations on the Department of Defense Information Network.
