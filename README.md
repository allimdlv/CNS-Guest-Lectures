##### **CPT Role in the MTS**


* **deployable units responsible for offering cybersecurity services to the Marine Transportation System**
* **three core CPT services: Assess, Hunt, Service**


##### **Mission Types**


* **Assess - guidance and recommendations to secure and protect MTS networks**
* **Hunt - discover undetected adversary on MTS before the compromise impacts critical services**
* **Incident Response - advise on mitigation \& remediation, forensic analysis, assistance with integration of FBI, CISA, and others**
